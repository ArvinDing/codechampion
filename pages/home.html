About Us

With a goal to make computer science, engineering, and technological learning accessible to any socioeconomic background, Code Champion is committed to educating and inspiring STEM for all students. Based in Orange County, California, Code Champion has provided free coding classes and organized computer science related events to hundreds of students from all around the world.

Code Champion is a President's Volunteer Service Award-certified non-profit under the incubation of Irvine Lights accepting volunteers from the United States only, no previous experience in computer science is required. For more information about our organization, visit our instagram @thecodechampion and if you have any questions for us, feel free to email them to codechampionus@gmail.com!
