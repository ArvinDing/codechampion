Our Mission

Code Champion is dedicated to educating all students in computer science regardless of social or financial status. Our goal is to bridge the gap in educational opportunities due to disparities in resources by training kids to be technology savvy at a young age. We are excited to teach students more about coding and we look forward to share our passion!
